About

Have you shopped online and wondered whether you're getting your money's worth? Or whether something was a scam? This website is not limited to just shopping online, but any service that you can rate. There have been times I wanted to try something new, but after looking at the reviews and ratings, I often think to myself: is it better for me to risk buying/ using this or should I keep my money?

If this happens to you, this website can help you with this. All you need to do is provide this site with the average rating given to a prodct/service and the number of people that left a rating, and we will calculate whether buying this would be a good idea.

Some good pointers to determine whether something is worth your money is listed below:

  • The number of ratings and views. (A product with a high rating with a low number of comments (e.g. 5 written reviews) is highly likely going to be a disappointment)
  • A product without a 1-star review is most likely a scam or (again) a disappointment. If you want this product, try and see if you can get something like this elsewhere and revaluate.
  • The average rating is below 4.4 stars. (This product/service could be good, but there are some annoyances you will encounter when using it).
  • Extreme contradictory reviews. (Reviews that favour the product/service (i.e. 5-star ratings describing an excellent experience) and reviews that are highly against it could indicate that the provider is trying to bump up its rating; this is disingenuous, and the product/service should be left alone).

r8d.uk calculates a score for this product by examining the ratio of the ratings to the number of reviews. For this product/service to be a good choice, at least 80% of the ratings must be 4 or 5 stars. But don't worry about the math as we'll do it for you.
